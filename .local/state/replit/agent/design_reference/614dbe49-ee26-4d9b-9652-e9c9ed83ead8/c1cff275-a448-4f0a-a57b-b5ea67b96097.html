What we're made of

Made with 100% ethically-sourced ingredients

Our condiments are crafted using only the finest, ethically-sourced ingredients, ensuring every bottle supports sustainable farming.

We use only pure, natural ingredients—no artificial preservatives, colors, or flavors—so you can taste the difference in every piece.

Eco-friendly packaging

Auntie is on a mission

Meet the heart and soul behind our authentic Indian condiments. Auntie's passion for traditional flavors and innovative combinations has created a line of condiments that bring families together around the dinner table.

With decades of experience in her family kitchen and restaurant, Auntie has perfected these recipes to share the authentic tastes of India with the world. Every bottle represents her commitment to quality, tradition, and the joy of sharing good food with loved ones.
